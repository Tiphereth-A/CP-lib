求 \(\displaystyle\begin{cases}
    \dfrac{\md}{\md x}f\equiv g(f)\pmod{x^n} \\
    f(0)=a
\end{cases}\)

\paragraph{输入}~\\

\verb|g|: \(g\), 调用方式为 \verb|poly g(poly f, size_t n)|, 其返回值为 \(g(f)\bmod{x^n}\)

\verb|dg|: \(g'\), 调用方式为 \verb|poly dg(poly f, size_t n)|, 其返回值为 \(g'(f)\bmod{x^n}\)

\verb|a|: \(a\)

\verb|n|: \(n\)

\paragraph{输出}

\(f\)

\paragraph{时间复杂度}

\(O(n\log n)\)
