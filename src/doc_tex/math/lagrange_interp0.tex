计算 \(f(x) \bmod m\), 其中 \(f(i) = v_i,~i = 0,1,\dots,|v|-1\)

\[
    \begin{aligned}
        f(x) & =\sum_{i=0}^{n-1} v_i \prod_{j\ne i} \frac{x-x_j}{x_i-x_j}                               \\
             & =\sum_{i=0}^{n-1} v_i \prod_{j\ne i} \frac{x-j}{i-j}                                     \\
             & =\sum_{i=0}^{n-1} (-1)^{n-i-1} v_i \frac{(\prod_{j<i} x-j)(\prod_{j>i} x-j)}{i!(n-i-1)!}
    \end{aligned}
\]

\paragraph{复杂度}

\(O(n)\)
